AM, IS or ARE?

Grammar mazes are a fun way of learning the difference between key elements of English grammar! In this version, decide whether the sentence is correct or not and, if not, what the error is. This can also be used as the basis for a traditional 'grammar auction' where student teams begin with an imaginary $500 and bet on whether sentences are correct or not.

She are happy because it is her birthday.

She is happy because today is her birthday
